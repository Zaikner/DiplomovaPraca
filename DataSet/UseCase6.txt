1. Customer logs in to the online store.
2. Customer searches for the desired product.
3. Online Store displays search results.
4. Customer selects a product from the search results.
5. Customer views product details, including price and availability.
6. Customer adds the product to their shopping cart.
7. Customer reviews the shopping cart and proceeds to checkout.
8. Customer provides shipping information.
9. Customer selects a payment method and provides payment details.
10. Online Store confirms the order and processes the payment.
11. Customer receives an order confirmation with the order number.
12. Online Store updates the inventory to reflect the purchase.
