1. User logs in to the calendar and scheduling application.
2. User selects the "Schedule Meeting" option.
3. User provides meeting details, including title, date, time, duration, and location.
4. User selects participants from their contacts or enters email addresses.
5. User sends meeting invitations to the selected participants.
6. Calendar and Scheduling Application schedules the meeting and sends invitations.
7. Participants receive meeting invitations via email.
8. Participants respond to the invitations (Accept, Decline, Tentative).
9. User and participants receive confirmation of responses.
