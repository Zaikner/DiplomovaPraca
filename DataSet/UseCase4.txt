1. Customer accesses the hotel booking system.
2. Customer enters the destination, check-in date, and check-out date.
3. Hotel booking system searches for available rooms within the specified date range.
4. Customer selects a room from the list of available options.
5. Customer provides guest information, including name and contact details.
6. Customer confirms the reservation.
7. Hotel booking system reserves the selected room.
8. Hotel booking system sends a confirmation to the customer via email.
