1. Customer logs in to the support system.
2. Customer navigates to the "Submit Support Ticket" section.
3. Customer provides a title and description of the technical issue.
4. Customer selects the priority level (e.g., low, medium, high) for the issue.
5. Customer attaches relevant files or screenshots.
6. Customer submits the support ticket.
7. Support System acknowledges receipt of the ticket and assigns a ticket number.
8. Support System sends an email confirmation to the customer with the ticket details.
9. Support System routes the ticket to the appropriate support team.
