1. Customer inserts their ATM card into the card reader.
2. ATM machine reads the card and prompts for a PIN (Personal Identification Number).
3. Customer enters their PIN.
4. ATM machine validates the PIN.
5. Customer selects the OBJECT option.
6. Customer specifies the amount of cash to withdraw.
7. ATM machine checks the account balance.
8. ATM machine dispenses the requested cash.
9. Customer takes the cash and the ATM card.
10. ATM machine updates the account balance.
