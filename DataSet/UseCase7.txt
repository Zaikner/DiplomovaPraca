1. Customer logs in to the online booking system.
2. Customer selects the "Book Flight" option.
3. Customer provides flight details, including departure and destination airports, departure date, and return date (if applicable).
4. Online Booking System searches for available flights based on the provided details.
5. Online Booking System displays a list of available flights.
6. Customer selects a specific flight from the list.
7. Customer provides passenger information, including names and contact details.
8. Customer selects any additional services (e.g., seat preferences, meal options).
9. Customer proceeds to payment.
10. Customer selects a payment method and provides payment details.
11. Online Booking System confirms the booking and issues a booking reference.
12. Customer receives a booking confirmation email with the booking reference.
13. Airline updates the seat inventory and booking records.
