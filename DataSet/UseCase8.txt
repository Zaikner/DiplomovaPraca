1. Employee logs in to the expense management system.
2. Employee navigates to the "Submit Expense Report" section.
3. Employee provides details for each expense, including the date, category, description, and amount.
4. Employee attaches scanned receipts or expense-related documents.
5. Employee submits the expense report.
6. Expense Management System acknowledges receipt and assigns a report number.
7. Employee's manager receives a notification for approval.
8. Manager reviews the expense report and either approves or rejects it.
9. If approved, the system processes the reimbursement.
10. Employee receives a reimbursement confirmation.
