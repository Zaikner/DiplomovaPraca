1. Customer selects the "Make Reservation" option.
2. System presents a list of available reservation dates and times.
3. Customer selects a date and time for the reservation.
4. System checks for table availability at the selected date and time.
5. Customer specifies the number of people in the party.
6. Customer confirms the reservation.
7. System sends a reservation request to the restaurant.
8. Restaurant staff receives the reservation request and checks availability.
9. If the reservation is confirmed, the restaurant staff updates the reservation status.
10. The system notifies the customer of the reservation confirmation.
